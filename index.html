Project

Project Quotation

Designed to facilitate the process of creating and managing price quotations for customers, the system employs a user-friendly interface that simplifies the quotation process. This user-friendly interface streamlines the process of generating price quotations for customers.

Login: The page that appears when the user wants to access the price quotation calculation information system by entering the registered email address and password.

Material: The page containing material data. Supervisor can insert, import, edit, and delete the material data.

Process: The page containing process data. Supervisor can insert, import, edit, and delete the process data.

Currency Slide: The page containing currency slide data. Supervisor can insert, import, edit, and delete the currency slide data.

Currency Non Slide: The page containing currency non slide data. Supervisor can insert, import, edit, and delete the currency non slide data.

Part Cost: The page containing part cost data. Staff can insert part cost data, calculate price quotation, and edit price quotation.

Calculate Price Quotation: The page contain empty price quotation form. Staff can calculate price quotation, view, and download price quotation to excel.

Edit Price Quotation: The page contain price quotation form that can be viewed or changed. Staff can edit price quotation, view, and download price quotation to excel.

Summary Part Cost: The page containing part cost data. Supervisor can edit and delete price quotation .
